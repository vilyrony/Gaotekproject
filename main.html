About the Consortium

The Consortium of Industrial IoT & M2M is a collaborative network of innovative companies dedicated to advancing industrial connectivity, automation, and machine-to-machine communication.

Our mission is to accelerate the deployment of scalable, high-performance solutions through strategic partnerships, shared expertise, and hardware-centric innovation, modernizing industrial ecosystems across North America.
