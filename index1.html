    Powering Real-World IIoT & M2M Deployment

    Industrial IoT (IIoT) and Machine-to-Machine (M2M) technologies are revolutionizing the way modern industries operate—enabling real-time data exchange, intelligent automation, and seamless communication between devices and systems. These technologies empower manufacturers, utilities, logistics providers, and infrastructure operators to optimize processes, reduce downtime, and make faster, data-driven decisions.

    At the heart of this transformation is a collaborative alliance of private technology, industrial, and manufacturing companies dedicated to advancing scalable, high-performance IIoT and M2M solutions. Through joint innovation, shared expertise, and real-world deployment, the group supports cross-sector progress in operational efficiency, safety, and digital integration. With a strong B2B presence and unified mission, the consortium fosters impactful partnerships, accelerates adoption of smart technologies, and helps shape the future of connected industry across North America.

    Key Applications

    Our consortium members collaborate to develop practical, high-impact applications across industries. These applications are designed to enhance productivity, reduce downtime, and streamline operations.

    About Us

    Driving Innovation in Industrial Connectivity and Machine-to-Machine Technologies

    The Consortium of Industrial IoT & M2M is a collective of forward-thinking companies dedicated to advancing industrial connectivity, automation, and machine-to-machine (M2M) communication. Our mission is to accelerate the integration and deployment of real-world solutions through strategic collaboration, shared expertise, and innovative hardware-centric development.

    We serve as a unified platform where technology providers, system integrators, and industrial partners collaborate to modernize infrastructure and scale smarter industrial ecosystems.
